Welcome to THE GLEAM HOTEL, your cozy and inviting retreat nestled in the scenic Engineer in Kinangop, Kenya. Since opening our doors in 2024, we have been dedicated to serving the community with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and a passion for great food. Our hotel offers a warm, friendly ambiance, perfect for catching up with friends over breakfast, enjoying a leisurely lunch, or sipping a cup of our signature artisanal coffee. Whether youre craving classic comfort food or exploring healthier, plant-based options,THE GLEAM HOTEL has something for everyone. Come in, relax, and let us brighten your day!
